# ILTMP (Ilmalaiva Texture Map)
Keys:
- _ - air
- ║ - vertical wall
- ═ - horizontal wall
- ╚ - wall corner (bottom-left)
- ╝ - wall corner (bottom-right)
- ╔ - wall corner (top-left)
- ╗ - wall corner (top-right)
- ¤ - wall with window
- E - wall with door
- ░ - floor
- P - player starting position (required)
- # - generator (required to win)
- @ - stove (required to win)
