# ILCMP (Ilmalaiva Collision Map)
Keys:
- X  - not passable (air)
- \# - collision (wall)
- 0  - passable (floor)
  - @ - generator

# ILTMP (Ilmalaiva Texture Map)
Keys:
- _ - air
- ║ - vertical wall
- ═ - horizontal wall
- ╚ - wall corner (bottom-left)
- ╝ - wall corner (bottom-right)
- ╔ - wall corner (top-left)
- ╗ - wall corner (top-right)
- ¤ - wall with window
- E - wall with door
- ░ - floor
- # - generator
- @ - stove
